\begin{abstract}
  Let $\left(f_{n}\right)_{n=0}^{\infty}$ be a sequence of
  \emph{contractions}. In this article  we investigate what conditions are
  necessary an sufficient for a sequence $\left(x_{n}\right)_{n=0}^{\infty}$,
  defined by $x_{0}\in\mathbb{R}$ and $x_{n+1}=f_{n}(x_{n})$, to converge.
\end{abstract}
